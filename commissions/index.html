The Process

General Timeline

  1. Come to me with idea/refs
  2. We discuss prices and confirm details
  3. Pay me the first part
  4. I come back with sketch (and keyframes if applicable)
  5. Iron out kinks
  6. I do the lineart
  7. Iron out minor details
  8. I do the animation
  9. Tweak timing n stuff

Edit Credit

After I finish a section of the commission, you are able to request edits to make the piece align more with your vision. Depending on what part I finished last, I will grant you a certian amount of "Edit Credits":

Phase Points
Sketch 10
Lineart 5
Animation 1

For major edits I will deduct two (2) credits, while for minor edits I will deduct one (1). If you run out of credits you can still request edits for a fee. More details under the "Additional Payments/Fees" section.

The Payment

Everything will be dealt with in USD. There will be one (1) payment per part: sketch, lineart, and animation. Whether it is all paid at once or only when it is time to work on the next phase, I will let you decide how and when you pay me.

Keep in mind that while I may still plan upcoming parts, I will not work on a specific one until payment is made.

I recommend all of these payments be made via paypal invoices, but if you prefer to use Commiss.io's system, that is perfectly fine.

If at anytime you want a refund, I'll return all the money for all parts I have not done as well as the completed parts of the project.

Additional Payments/Fees

About Payment Processing

You are only paying for my time, my expertise, and your product: You do not have to fret about including payment processing fees; I will take the brunt of that.

Extra Edit Fees

Asking for edits beyond the intitally given ones may incur an "Extra Edit" fee. If there are any remaining Edit Credits, they will be used to lower the fee of a major edit to the cost of a minor edit. The cost of said fee is determined by what part I am working on:

Phase Minor Major
Sketch ¢25 ¢50
Lineart $1 $2
Animation $2.50 $5

I will only work on the additional edit after I recive payment for the fee. Please note that after I make the edit, the fee is no long refundable.

Only during the sketch phase, if you send me an image of my sketch which the changes you want highlighted, then I will take ¢25 off of the edit fee.

1 Items listed are subject to change at any time.
